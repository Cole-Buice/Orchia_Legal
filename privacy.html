Privacy Policy

Orchia — Last updated April 2026

1. Overview

This policy explains what information Orchia collects, how it is used, and what we do not do with it. We are committed to collecting only what is necessary and handling it responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, chosen username, and date of birth.

Age verification: Your date of birth is stored securely on our servers and is used solely to determine your age bracket (for example, 13–15, 16–17, 18–21, 21+). Your raw date of birth is never returned to the app or shared with anyone. Only your age bracket is used by the app to apply appropriate settings.

App data: Tasks, schedules, and other content you create are stored both on your device and synced to our servers. A small number of device-specific preferences (such as notification and display settings) are stored locally only. All account-linked data, including your tasks, recurring schedules, and app history, is stored server-side.

Friends and sharing: If you use the friends feature, your username is searchable by other users. Task sharing sends only the task content you choose to share.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify your age and apply appropriate safety settings
  • To sync your app data across sessions
  • To provide and improve the service

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share your date of birth with anyone, including other users
  • We do not use your information for any purpose beyond operating the service

5. Data Storage

The majority of your data, including tasks, schedules, and account information, is stored on our servers using Supabase, a third-party database provider. A small amount of device-specific preferences is stored locally on your device only. We take reasonable steps to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Orchia is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly permit children under 13 to create accounts. If we become aware that an account belongs to a user under 13, we will terminate it. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us.

For users aged 13–17, we apply additional privacy protections through age-bracket-based feature gating. Social and sharing features may be limited for younger users.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app. You may also contact us to request a copy of your data or to correct inaccurate information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us through the app or at the support address listed on our app store page.
