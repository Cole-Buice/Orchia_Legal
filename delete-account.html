Delete Your Account

Orchia

You can delete your Orchia account and all associated data directly from within the app. This will permanently remove your account, tasks, schedules, and personal information from our servers.

How to delete your account in the app:

  1. Open the Orchia app and log in
  2. Tap the menu icon to open the side drawer
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our servers. If you have an active subscription, cancel it through the Play Store before deleting your account.

Deleting individual data without closing your account

You can delete specific data at any time without deleting your account:

  • Tasks — swipe to delete or use the task menu on any individual task
  • Recurring schedules — open the schedule and select Delete from the options menu
  • Completed and deleted tasks — cleared from the completed and deleted task history screens

Deleted content is removed from your device and our servers.

If you are unable to access the app and need assistance deleting your account or data, contact us at the support address listed on our Play Store listing.
